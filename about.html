About Suruchi
Our story, values and the people behind the food.

Our Name, Our Promise

The name Suruchi (सुरुचि) means “Good Taste.” It is the promise we make to every guest: genuine flavours, fresh ingredients and warm hospitality. We celebrate North Indian & Mughlai cooking using time-honoured techniques such as slow dum and clay tandoor roasting.



Our Kitchen Team
